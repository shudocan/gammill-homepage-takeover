The Gammill service network

A nationwide team of factory-trained Gammill technicians - and a warranty that actually means something.

When you call about your machine, the person on the other end of the line works for Gammill. When a tech drives to your house, they work for Gammill. Trained by us, certified by us, paid by us, accountable to us. No "authorized" middlemen. No franchise inconsistencies. No "your dealer can help with that."

And when you need help, you have three ways to reach a Gammill tech: phone, live chat, or a pick-a-time Zoom video meeting - staffed 7 days a week, 12 hours a day. Zoom appointments typically book the same day with a real tech support rep. Not a ticket queue. Not a callback window. Same day.

And we service the whole system in your studio. When other longarm machines need work, owners often have to remove the head from its table and ship it back to a factory - which means the table, frame, and drive geometry never get diagnosed alongside the head. Repairs come back incomplete. Root causes that live in the relationship between the parts get missed. Gammill techs work on the whole system in place, where it lives, the way it's used.

This is the only service model in the longarm industry. It's the reason our limited lifetime warranty isn't a marketing line - it's a promise the people fulfilling it have a paycheck riding on.

A note on the "your dealer's right around the corner" pitch.

When a competitor leans hard on how close their nearest dealer is, please - listen to what they're actually telling you. The reason a dealer needs to be close is that their machine is going to need that help. The harder they push the dealer-proximity story, the more they're telling you, without realizing it: that machine breaks, and often.

Gammill's technicians cover every region of the U.S. by design - not by necessity. The recommended professional tune-up is once every five years or 500 quilts. Service is the safety net, not the lifeline.

  • Field technicians directly employed by Gammill - every region of the U.S.
  • Trained, certified, and paid by Gammill - not a third party
  • Phone, live chat, or same-day Zoom video - direct to a Gammill tech, 7 days a week, 12 hours a day
  • Owner-maintainable. Wearable parts come with online walkthroughs - you can replace them yourself without a tech visit
  • Limited lifetime warranty on every new machine - and it doesn't shrink if you quilt for customers (many competitor warranties do)
  • Every part is replaceable. When the time comes, a Gammill retrofit brings any older machine back to current-model spec

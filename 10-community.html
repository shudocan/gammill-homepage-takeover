Why Gammill - Chapter 10

You're not just buying a machine. You're joining a family.

A longarm sits in a quilter's home for decades. The network of people who own one keeps showing up for each other - at Quilting with Confidence events, and in the personal homes where the next Gammill owner gets her first look at a Gammill. No other longarm brand has anything close to this community.

Owner to owner. By design.

There's a good chance another Gammill owner is the reason you're reading this.

We don't sell through dealers anymore. There was no way to control fraudulent and illegal activity because they were independent. All machine orders are now handled through the home office, delivered, serviced, and trained by Gammill factory staff. But the people who show off Gammill machines in their own studios and living rooms - we call them Super Fans - are Gammill owners themselves. At some point in the past they had a friend introduce them to the Gammill machine that changed their world, and now they've decided to pay it forward, the same way somebody once did for them.

That's not a marketing program. That's the network. And it doesn't end at the sale - Quilting with Confidence brings hundreds of Gammill owners together every year. Regional retreats run year-round. The owners' online community is one of the most active in longarm. PatternCloud connects every Statler, Ascend, and Elevate to a constantly-growing library of new designs from working designers.

  • Owner-to-owner introductions - no commissioned dealer network
  • Quilting with Confidence - annual owners' event
  • Regional retreats and educator-led workshops year-round
  • Active owners' online community
  • PatternCloud - direct-to-machine pattern library
  • The Accelerate Program for owners running a quilting business
"It feels like I've come home."
Althea Smith · Statler owner · Yorkshire Dales, UK

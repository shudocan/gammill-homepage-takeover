How we make that promise

Built on a foundation that can't wear out.

At the center of every Gammill machine is a set of hydrodynamically lubricated journal bearings, with clear, non-staining oil delivered by a network of little hoses. Industrial equipment since the 1880s has relied on this type of bearing because of its durability.

What does wear out over decades is everything electrical - motors, electronics, controls, wiring. Even when those electrical parts age out, the machine isn't worn out, and neither is the steel table the machine rests on. You keep the solid structure of the Gammill, because it's still working like new. The electronics are replaceable (more on that ahead).

Every Gammill built since 1997 is still in service today. No other longarm manufacturer can say that. It's also why pre-owned Gammills hold the highest resale value in the longarm industry.

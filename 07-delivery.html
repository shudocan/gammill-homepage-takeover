Delivered, set up, dialed in

Your delivery is a substantial visit by a Gammill expert in getting you launched properly, not a curbside drop.

A Gammill machine isn't shipped to your driveway in a box for you to assemble. It's installed in your home - by a full-time, salaried Gammill delivery technician (not a part-timer) - as part of the price of the machine. They arrive on schedule, bring out your old machine if you have one, and stay until your Gammill is in your space, level, calibrated, and ready to stitch.

During the visit, the technician customizes the setup to your room: working height, lighting, table location, and floor flatness. Even on a sloped basement floor or a second-story room with seasonal flex, they level the frame to true. Then they walk you through your first stitches, hands-on, until you're comfortable using the basic functions.

By the time the technician leaves, your machine isn't a project - it's a working tool. Most owners are quilting their first real piece on Day 1.

  • 100% delivered and set up by Gammill technicians (not third-party freight)
  • Customized to your room: height, lighting, table location, floor leveling
  • Hands-on first-stitch training before the technician leaves
  • Old machine removed if you have one
  • Included in the price for the lower 48 (extra charge outside Continental US)

Statler · Statler Ascend

All the tools shipped on day one. None of it locked behind a tier.

CreativeStudio handles the full vocabulary of computerized quilting: edge-to-edge, blocks, repeat patterns, point-to-point, echo, fill, draw trims, border corners, resize, rotate, flip, mirror, nodes, circular array, combine, and order-join. None of it sold separately. None of it locked behind a tier. None of it added in a paid upgrade later.

Statler Ascend adds 360° channel lock - the spec where Ascend pulls ahead of Statler. The rest of the feature set is identical between Statler and Ascend, which means your training and your library follow you if you upgrade.

And the upgrades stay free, forever. Gammill has never charged for CreativeStudio - or for any upgrade since the software was first released. A 2008 Statler runs the same CreativeStudio as a 2026 Ascend. Updates are free to every machine owner.

  • CreativeStudio at the stitcher (Statler, Statler Ascend)
  • Pattern preview - see exactly where stitches will land before the first stitch (great for getting customer approval before you commit)
  • Resilient workflow - auto-restart at exact pattern position after a thread break or bobbin change; auto-calculate rows, repeats, and pattern fit from a single measurement
  • Stand-Alone CreativeStudio works with any computerized model - including Elevate. Design and plan your next quilt while the current one is still being stitched with SAM
  • 25+ years of continuous improvement driven by owner feedback and requests
  • Every Gammill owner runs the same software - newbie or 20-year veteran - which is what lets the community help each other

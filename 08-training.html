New Owner Training · Included

Training that doesn't end at delivery.

Every new Gammill comes with the New Owner Training Program - a 24/7 online portal of short, focused video lessons (about 4 minutes each on average), checklists, and walkthroughs you can work through at your own pace. Some learning happens while you're waiting for the machine to arrive. Other topics make more sense months later, when you want to attempt a new technique. It's like learning your machine in your pajamas - and you have access forever.

Beyond the on-demand library: monthly live Q&A sessions with Gammill educators, and two personal training sessions tailored to your machine, your needs, and your goals. You're never dependent on a single instructor - if one teacher's style doesn't click for you, there are more on the next video, the next Q&A, the next session. The Education hub is open to every owner the day they buy.

  • 24/7 New Owner Training Program - videos and walkthroughs at your pace, forever
  • Two personal training sessions, one-on-one with a Gammill educator
  • Monthly live Q&A with Gammill educators
  • Gammill Video Series - owners' on-demand library

New Owner Training · Included

Training that doesn't end at delivery.

Every new Gammill comes with the New Owner Training Program - a 24/7 online portal of videos, checklists, and walkthroughs you can work through at your own pace. Some owners learn the software while waiting for the machine to arrive. Others come back to the library months later when they want to attempt a new technique. It's like learning your machine in your pajamas - and it never goes away.

Beyond the on-demand library: monthly live Q&A sessions with Gammill educators, and two personal training sessions tailored to your machine, your space, and your goals. Quilting with Confidence is the annual in-person owners' event. Regional retreats run year-round. The Education hub is open to every owner the day they buy.

  • 24/7 New Owner Training Program - videos and walkthroughs at your pace, forever
  • Two personal training sessions, one-on-one with a Gammill educator
  • Monthly live Q&A with Gammill educators
  • Quilting with Confidence - annual in-person owners' event
  • Regional retreats, year-round
  • Gammill Video Series - owners' on-demand library
  • Phone support during business hours, by a real human

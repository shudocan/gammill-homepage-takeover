Integrated pattern management

A library that finds patterns, organizes them, and backs them up - from the same screen you stitch from.

PatternCloud is more than a store. It's the pattern brain of every computerized Gammill - a curated, designer-driven library of more than 14,000 quilting patterns that you can browse, search, preview, purchase, and load directly from your machine. No flash drives. No file conversions. No "where did I save that pantograph" moments.

When a designer publishes a new pattern, it appears on your machine. Search by designer, type, or tag. Filter by what fits your block. Preview before you buy. Once it's yours, it's organized - and securely backed up to the cloud, so you never lose your library when a hard drive dies or a machine swaps hands.

PatternCloud also follows you across machines. Use Stand-Alone CreativeStudio on a laptop to lay out a quilt at the kitchen table, then load the layout onto your Gammill in the studio. The patterns travel with the account, not the device.

  • 14,000+ patterns, growing weekly
  • Browse, search, purchase - without leaving CreativeStudio
  • Organize by designer, type, tags
  • Secure cloud backup of your entire library
  • Multi-machine access (e.g., your stand-alone laptop)
  • 1,000+ digital patterns included on every Statler & Statler Ascend

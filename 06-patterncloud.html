Integrated pattern management

A library that finds patterns, organizes them, and backs them up - from the same screen you stitch from.

PatternCloud is more than a store. It's the pattern brain of every computerized Gammill - a curated, designer-driven library of more than 20,000 quilting patterns that you can browse, search, preview, purchase, and load directly from your machine. No flash drives. No file conversions. No "where did I save that downloaded file?" moments.

When a designer publishes a new pattern or improves an old one, it appears on your machine as if by magic. Search by designer, type, or tag. Filter by what fits your block or quilt. Preview before you buy, right on your quilt. Once it's yours, it's organized - and securely backed up to the cloud, so you never lose your library when a hard drive dies. And PatternCloud stops you from buying the same pattern twice.

PatternCloud also follows you across machines. Use Stand-Alone CreativeStudio on a laptop to lay out a quilt at the kitchen table, then load the layout onto your Gammill in the studio. And when you're visiting friends or family across the country - or around the world - your patterns are at your fingertips even on a Gammill that isn't yours. The patterns travel with you.

  • 20,000+ patterns, growing weekly
  • Browse, search, purchase - without leaving CreativeStudio
  • Organize by designer, type, tags
  • Secure cloud backup of your entire library
  • Multi-machine access - share to all your machines, plus your stand-alone laptop
  • 1,200+ digital patterns included on every Statler & Statler Ascend

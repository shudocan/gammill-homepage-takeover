Why Gammill — Chapter 6

You're
never alone.

Picture this: a longarm machine shows up at your house in a dozen boxes. The unboxing isn't the hard part, and even assembly isn't the worst — ruining quilts is what's hard. So Gammill is with you every step of the way.

New Owner Training · Included

Delivery, installation, and training — included.

Every new Gammill comes with delivery, installation, and New Owner Training included in the price. A trained Gammill technician sets up the machine in your space, levels the table, calibrates the system, walks you through the first stitches, and reviews basic machine operation with you, hands-on. You'll be quilting actual quilts — for yourself or for customers — from Day 1.

After that, education doesn't stop. Quilting with Confidence is our annual owner event. Regional retreats run year-round. The Gammill Video Series and Education hub are open to every owner the day they buy.

  • In-home delivery and setup (extra charge outside Continental US)
  • Comprehensive New Owner Training included
  • Gammill Video Series — owners' on-demand library
  • Phone support during business hours, by a real human

Why Gammill - Chapter 11

You're not just buying a machine. You're joining a family.

Longarm quilting can be lonely. Most quilters work in a spare room or basement, alone, hours from anyone else who does what they do. A Gammill comes with a network of people who refuse to let you stay isolated - at Quilting with Confidence events, and in the living rooms where owners introduce the next Gammill owner. No other longarm brand has anything close to this community.

Owner to owner. By design.

There's a good chance another Gammill owner is the reason you're reading this.

We don't sell through dealers anymore, and there's no commissioned sales force. The people who show off Gammill machines in their own studios and living rooms - we call them Super Fans - are Gammill owners themselves. At some point in the past they had a friend introduce them to the Gammill machine that changed their world, and now they've decided to pay it forward, the same way somebody once did for them.

All machine orders are handled through the home office, delivered, serviced, and trained by Gammill factory staff. We moved to a direct-from-factory model so every owner gets the same quality of attention from day one. Which also means the price is the price. Same machine, same price, same experience - regardless of your zip code or your negotiating skills.

That's the family you're joining. And this family feeling doesn't end at the sale - our live events are legendary:

  • Quilting with Confidence brings hundreds of Gammill owners together every year.
  • Gammill Quilting Institute's Regional Retreats run on alternating years.
  • Gammill Cruises - hang out in exotic locations with your Gammill family.
  • Online, the Gammill owners' community is one of the most active in the longarm world.
"It feels like I've come home."
Althea Smith · Statler owner · Yorkshire Dales, UK
